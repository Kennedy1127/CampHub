STEP 01 . Reservation

  1. 1.

    Choose a campground: Look for campgrounds that fit your needs, such as tent camping, RV camping, and price of each campground.

  2. 2.

    Make a reservation: Provide your personal information, such as your name, address, and phone number, as well as the dates you want to stay and the type of camping you prefer.

  3. 3.

    Pay for your reservation: We require full payment at the time of reservation. Pay for your reservation according to the CampHub's policies.

STEP 02 . Access

  1. ＃ACCESSED BY CAR

    1. 1.

      Plan your route: Use a GPS or map to determine the best way to get here.

    2. 2.

      Check the weather: Preparing for any weather-related hazards or concerns.

    3. 3.

      Pack your gear: Pack all the gear and supplies you will need for your camping trip.

  2. ＃ACCESSED BY TRAIN

    1. 1.

      Research train schedules: Determine the best time to arrive and depart, and plan your trip accordingly.

    2. 2.

      Walk to the CampHub: Follow any walking or hiking trails that lead to the CampHub.

  3. ＃ACCESSED BY BUS

    1. 1.

      Take the bus: Take the bus to the nearest bus stop to the CampHub.

    2. 2.

      Follow any walking or hiking trails that lead to the CampHub, and be sure to stay on designated trails to avoid damaging the environment.
